TERMies — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information TERMies (“the App,” “we,” “us”), operated by Jeremiah York, collects when you use it inside Discord, how we use that information, and the choices you have. By using TERMies you agree to this policy.

TERMies is an independent project. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Discord Inc., or by Wordle or The New York Times Company.

1. Information we collect

To run the game and its leaderboards, we collect and store:

  • Discord account identifiers — your Discord user ID, username, and avatar, as provided by Discord when you launch or interact with the App.
  • Server identifiers — the IDs of the Discord servers (guilds) where you play, so we can build server leaderboards.
  • Gameplay data — your guesses, results, scores, number of attempts, streaks, and timestamps for each word you play.
  • Social data — the in-app friend connections you create (friend requests you send, accept, or remove).

2. What we do NOT collect

  • We do not read or store the content of your messages. The App does not use Discord’s Message Content intent.
  • We do not collect your email address, password, payment information, or real-world identity.
  • We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

3. How we use your information

  • To run the game: track your progress, score words, and maintain your stack.
  • To build server, friends, weekly, and all-time leaderboards and your personal stats.
  • To operate the friends system you opt into.
  • To prevent cheating, abuse, and to keep the service working (e.g. rate limiting).

4. How your information is shared

We don’t sell your data. We share it only:

  • With other players, by design — your username and scores appear on leaderboards visible to people in your server or to your friends. Result grids are shown as colored squares only, never the words.
  • With service providers — our hosting/database provider stores the data on our behalf.
  • For legal reasons — if required by law or to protect rights, safety, or the integrity of the service.

5. Data retention & deletion

We keep your gameplay data for as long as you use TERMies. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at support@pickaxesound.com. We will delete your records within a reasonable period, except where we must retain limited information to comply with the law.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live (for example under GDPR or the CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@pickaxesound.com.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure. The day’s answer is never sent to your device until your attempt is complete.

8. Children

TERMies is not directed to children under 13, and you must meet Discord’s minimum age requirement (13+, or older where required in your country) to use it.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will revise the “Effective date” above and, for material changes, provide notice where practical. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions or requests: support@pickaxesound.com.
